Evacuating

With the unpredictable nature of storms, staying informed is one of the most important aspects of enhancing safety. After preparing supplies and packing for a potential evacuation, staying alert on the situation of flooding in the area is key. In order to stay alert, it would be beneficial to pay attention to news coverage in the area, one such reliable news source for keeping track of weather conditions is KSBW8.

This news station has relevant information on weather conditions that is updated consistently and covers reports across Monterey County, as well as surrounding areas. Their website is easily accessible, and they can also be found on various social media apps, like Instagram.

A more direct resource that can help keep you informed on storm warnings, and specifically evacuations is the Monterey County maps arc gis app. This site is updated regularly and provides topical information in relation to flooding, including whether areas are under evacuation, not at risk for evacuation, or in an evacuation warning phase. The map also includes information on shelters, road closures, and sandbag stockpiles in Monterey County. Having the link to this site bookmarked or easily accessible on a mobile phone is a great way of ensuring you will know when the proper time to evacuate will be.
